How to play

Guess the 5‑letter word in 6 tries. Each guess must be a real word. After you submit, the tiles will show how close your guess was:

  • W Correct letter in the right spot.
  • I Letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • N Letter is not in the word.

Try Hard Mode to lock in revealed hints. Share your score with friends!
